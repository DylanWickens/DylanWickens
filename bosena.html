We designed an identity for the Cornish production company Bosena, whose work explores the craft, and appreciation of film as a cultural act. The identity itself is heavily inspired by the intricacies of Celtic weaving and knotwork, aspiring to create a cohesive visual that not only reinforces Bosena’s holistic approach to the art of film, but encourages traditional cultural iconography; a reminder of the ‘true’ origins of the company itself.
